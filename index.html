About

Hello! I'm Isaac Velez, a first year student of Data Science & Business Analytics (London School of Economics) and Business & Leadership (Forward College).

I co-developed Stay Focused, an open-source app that helped Marlon, a 38-year-old man with a psychiatric disability, maintain his job at a grocery store. Additionally, I have collaborated on projects with a company called Bonnett Analytics. I also developed a chatbot with Auteco Mobility and Trakku, allowing users to remotely disable their motorcycles in the event of theft.
